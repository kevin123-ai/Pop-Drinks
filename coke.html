Coke


Coke, also known as Coca-Cola, is a carbonated soft drink that was first created in 1886 by John Stith Pemberton. It is one of the most popular and recognizable brands in the world. Coke is known for its sweet and refreshing taste, and it is often enjoyed on its own or used as a mixer in various cocktails.

The drink is made with a combination of carbonated water, high fructose corn syrup, caramel color, phosphoric acid, natural flavors, and caffeine.

Coke has a rich history and has become a cultural icon over the years. It has been associated with various advertising campaigns, sponsorships, and events. The brand has also expanded its product line to include different flavors and variations, such as Diet Coke, Coke Zero, and Cherry Coke.

Overall, Coke is a beloved beverage that has stood the test of time and continues to be enjoyed by millions of people around the world.
