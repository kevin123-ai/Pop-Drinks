Pepsi


Pepsi is a carbonated soft drink that was first created in 1893 by Caleb Bradham.

It is known for its sweet and refreshing taste, making it a popular choice among consumers worldwide.

Pepsi has a strong global presence and is available in various flavors and formulations, including diet and caffeine-free options.

The brand has a long history of advertising and marketing campaigns, often featuring popular celebrities and catchy slogans.

Pepsi continues to innovate and evolve, introducing new flavors and limited-edition releases to cater to changing consumer preferences.
